About Me

The story behind the developer

Hey, I'm Aryan Kumar. I'm currently in my diploma in AI and Machine Learning, and I spend a lot of my free time building things — mostly web apps, Android apps, and Python projects.

My most serious project so far is an AI Road Crossing Assistant for visually impaired people. It actually won 3rd place at an international symposium, which was a big deal for me. Most of what I learn comes from building stuff, breaking it, and figuring out why it broke.

I'm still learning a lot. I try to work across different areas — AI/ML, full-stack web, and Android — so I can build complete end-to-end things rather than just one piece. I enjoy open-source and like seeing how other developers approach problems.
